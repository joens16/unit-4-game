Dragon Ball Game
Bring Dragon to Life

You will be give a random number at the beginning. By clicking on a Dragon Ball you will add points to your total score. You will win the game by matching your total score to random number. you lose if your total score goes above the random number. The value of each Bragon Ball is hidden from you until you click on it. each time when the game starts, the value of each dragon Ball will change.
